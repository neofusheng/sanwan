Day 3. Chinese New Year's Eve.

I told the AI: "Send a personalized New Year greeting to every one of my 611 employees."

I reviewed a few. Approved. It sent them all. From my bed.

**Tweet 5 (It gets real)**

By Day 7, things got weird — in a good way.

The AI started managing its own task queue. Multiple agents began coordinating. It remembered my preferences across sessions.

I wasn't giving commands anymore. I was having conversations.

Day 11. I said: "Build me a website."

You don't need to code.
