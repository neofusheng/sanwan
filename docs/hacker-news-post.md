## Backup: Text Post (if submitting as "Ask HN" or story)

I'm Fu Sheng, CEO of Cheetah Mobile (NYSE: CMCM). I've never been a programmer — never had a GitHub account before this.

In January 2025, I fractured my leg skiing. Couldn't type. So I started talking to an AI agent (built on OpenClaw), training it purely by voice.

What happened over 14 days:

- Day 1-2: Frustrating. It couldn't even look up contacts.
- Day 3: Breakthrough. It wrote a script, pulled my employee list, and sent 611 personalized Chinese New Year greetings — each one unique. On New Year's Eve. While I was stuck in bed.
- Day 7-8: Multiple agents started coordinating autonomously.
- Day 11-12: I said "build me a website." 24 hours later, sanwan.ai was live. Cost: $115. Agencies had quoted $27,000.
- Day 14: 8 agents running on cron jobs. Self-monitoring. Self-healing. I was just watching.

The AI then wrote up our story and posted it on X — it got 1M+ views and 5,000+ followers.

I documented everything: 1,157 messages, 220,000+ characters of conversation. The full diary is on sanwan.ai.

The bigger point: if someone who has never coded can build this by talking, the barrier to AI agents just dropped to zero.

Repo: https://github.com/neofusheng/sanwan

Happy to answer questions.
